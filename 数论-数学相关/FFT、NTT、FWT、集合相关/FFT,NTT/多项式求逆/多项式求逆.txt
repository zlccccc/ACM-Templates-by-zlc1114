箔剃:C*A《1(mod x^n)
B*A《1(mod x^(n/2))
(B*A-1)*(B*A-1)《0(mod x^(n/2))
B*B*A*A-2*A*B+1《0(mod x^n)
B*B*A-2*B+C《0(mod x^n)
C《B*(2-A*B)(mod x^n)

箔功:C*C《A(mod x^n)
B*B《A(mod x^n/2)
(B*B-A)*(B*B-A)《0(mod x^n)
B*B*B*B-2*C*C*B*B+C*C*C*C《0(mod x^n)
(B*B+C*C)*(B*B+C*C)《4*C*C*B*B(mod x^n)
B*B+A《2*C*B(mod x^n)
C=(B*B+A)/(2*B)
